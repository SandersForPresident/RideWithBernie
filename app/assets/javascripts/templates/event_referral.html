Get your rideshare link!

1 Find your event on the map.
2 Click the blue CARPOOL button.
3 Copy/Save the URL in your browser's address bar. That's your rideshare link!
2 Post/share the rideshare link for attendees to sign up.
4 Attendees will sign up and send their contact info through the site, then coordinate ridesharing via text/phone!
